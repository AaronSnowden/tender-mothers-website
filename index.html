Empowering Communities

Our Mission

To have healthy and happy children, with fully independent mothers

Our Vision

To have a world of happy mothers and children

What We Do

To fight malaria, pneumonia and malnutrition among children through various interventions and partnerships.

To emphasize on hygiene among teenage mothers.

To fight school dropping out among school going teenage girls and boys.

To identify and provide basic needs of life to mothers and children.

To increase access to education among children by intervention to fight any barriers.

To empower and equip teenage mothers with hands on technical skills.
