Who we are

Tender Mothers is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting mothers in rural communities through health, education, and economic empowerment. We work directly with local health workers, educators, and volunteers to provide life-changing support.

Our Mission

To have healthy and happy children, with fully independent mothers.
